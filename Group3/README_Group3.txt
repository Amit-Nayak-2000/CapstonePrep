To run our program, grind your coffee beans.
Then, navigate to the MATLAB folder (double click the folder "MATLAB", in Matlab).
Open GUI.m using MATLAB and then click run. 
This will open the GUI in a new window.
Set the patient parameters and click BUILD.
Brew your coffee according to your preference.
By the time the coffee is done brewing, the code should have finished running.
Drink your coffee while you enjoy our SOLIDWORKS.
Click Exit Designer to close the GUI.
